Services  Enterprise Agile

Keep pace with changing trends with enterprise agility

Talk to our experts

Business landscapes and marketplaces are evolving at an unprecedented pace. Structures, systems, and ways of working built and reinforced over the century, are struggling to keep up with the demands placed upon them. In this scenario, there is an urgent need to switch to agile ways of working to be able to turn on a dime, be operationally efficient and deliver at an unprecedented pace.

We help our customers accomplish change by taking a holistic view, focusing on four dimensions: People, Practices, Technology, and Organization. We understand that there is no one-size-fits-all solution. We therefore leverage our years of extensive experience with agile delivery to enable your Enterprise Transformation journey from adoption to implementation of end-to-end Enterprise Agility with our customised solutions which are agnostic of company size and domain.

what we do

Training & Coaching

Our specialized training, certifications, and programs include:

  • SAFe and ICAgile
  • Business and Leadership Agility
  • Sessions on Team Level Agility (with Scrum, Lean, Kanban, XP)
  • Specialized workshops for Contract Management, Design Thinking, Agile Estimation, Technical Agility, Agile Health Check and programs like Agile Challenge
  • Role-based training programs for Scrum Masters, Product Owner, and team

Agile Consulting

Specialized and bespoke advice to address our customer’s specific needs over implementing all best practices of Agility and DevOps. We offer:

  • Proficiency assessments like Leadership Agility Self-assessment, Enterprise Agility Assessment, & Team Agility Assessment
  • Start-up kit for easy adoption of Agile practices
  • Agile Experience Lab for Agile Product Management and enabling pilot Agile teams for early and continuous delivery of value
  • HR agility: Translate agile values to HR projects

Adaption & Transformation

  • Transformation Readiness Assessments for evaluating current state and readiness for initiating transformation journey with our customized Agile Transformation Workshop
  • Organizational Design for structuring the organization to accelerate value delivery
  • Strategy Workshop with executives to lay out transformation strategy
